About 70mai Jordan

We are "Adsat Al-Tariq for Automotive Technology", the first institution in Jordan specialized in dashcams (car recording cameras), licensed by the Ministry of Industry and Trade, the Public Security Directorate and the Ministry of Interior, to practice the retail trade of surveillance camera systems, and is an official agent for the products of (70mai) Company "one of the strategic partners of Xiaomi Company - (Xiaomi)". As this institution came after the essence of real experiences and engineering expertise in the same field, to provide the best products that keep pace with the requirements of customers to protect their vehicles and provide fleet services and track vehicles with the best modern means and technologies such as "dashcam" and cameras that contain tracking devices that are placed inside the vehicle compartment with engineering and scientific supply and installation services at the vehicle site in accordance with the technical requirements of the vehicle manufacturing companies and without violating the conditions of public safety and the manufacturer's warranty for vehicles from the agent of the manufacturing companies
